# Retail Growth Pulse

Use for commercial tables where the central questions are growth, mix, conversion, refund, and product/channel opportunities.

## Best Fit

- ecommerce order exports
- shop/platform sales tables
- campaign order reports
- product sales snapshots

## Report Modules

1. GMV/order/customer/AOV KPI strip
2. Sales trend
3. Product/category/channel contribution
4. Refund or abnormal order analysis
5. Growth opportunity cards
6. Commercial action plan

## Visual Grammar

Warm light canvas, coral/teal/blue/violet accents, energetic product cards, contribution bars, opportunity badges.

## Recommendation Style

Commercial and direct: adjust campaign, replenish stock, push high-performing SKU, investigate high-refund product, localize channel strategy.
