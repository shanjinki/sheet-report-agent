# Executive Command Center

Use for leadership-facing operational or risk reports where the audience needs to know what is healthy, what is blocked, and what decisions are needed.

## Best Fit

- DevOps/project demand lists
- support and operations tables
- risk or exception registers
- status-heavy trackers

## Report Modules

1. Executive status headline
2. KPI row for total, healthy/completed, backlog/open, blocked/risk, in progress
3. Lifecycle or status funnel
4. Category health grid sorted by risk
5. Owner/load or team concentration
6. Risk list and decision requests

## Visual Grammar

Dark navy, cyan/emerald/amber/red accents, dense but organized cards, glowing borders, compact bars and funnels. Use color as status language.

## Recommendation Style

Short, urgent, action-oriented: coordinate resources, unblock stage, prune backlog, reassign owner, decide pause/continue.
