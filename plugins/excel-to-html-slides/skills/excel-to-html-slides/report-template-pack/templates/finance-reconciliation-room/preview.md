# Finance Reconciliation Room

Use when finance tables are exception-heavy and need a sharper control-room treatment.

## Best Fit

- reconciliation mismatch sheets
- unpaid invoice exports
- payment aging tables
- finance operations exceptions

## Report Modules

1. Total amount and unresolved amount
2. Match/mismatch status
3. Aging buckets
4. Vendor/customer/category concentration
5. High-value exception list
6. Reconciliation action plan

## Visual Grammar

Command-center but finance-dense: dark cards, aging bars, high-value exception panels, controlled severity colors.

## Recommendation Style

Precise and accountable: reconcile, collect evidence, assign owner, clear aging bucket, confirm accounting treatment.
