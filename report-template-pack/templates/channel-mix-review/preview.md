# Channel Mix Review

Use when a table contains source/channel/platform/region dimensions and a contribution metric.

## Best Fit

- sales by channel
- leads by source
- ecommerce platform comparisons
- regional operation tables

## Report Modules

1. Total metric and top channel contribution
2. Channel/region/category mix
3. Concentration or dependency risk
4. Underperforming channel list
5. Growth opportunity list
6. Channel action plan

## Visual Grammar

Retail-pulse with contribution bars, mix cards, colorful channel tags, opportunity highlights.

## Recommendation Style

Commercial: invest, prune, localize, improve conversion, investigate weak channel quality.
