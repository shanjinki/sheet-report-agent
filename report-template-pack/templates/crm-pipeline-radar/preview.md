# CRM Pipeline Radar

Use for sales pipeline and opportunity tables where stage movement and stale deals matter.

## Best Fit

- CRM lead exports
- opportunity pipeline sheets
- sales forecast tables
- account follow-up trackers

## Report Modules

1. Leads/opportunities/pipeline amount
2. Stage funnel
3. Source or channel quality
4. Sales owner workload and conversion
5. Stale or no-next-step opportunity list
6. Forecast risk and follow-up plan

## Visual Grammar

Boardroom-light base, funnel chart, owner comparison cards, amber stale-risk badges, high-value opportunity table.

## Recommendation Style

Sales-management focused: update next action, clean stage, prioritize large stuck deals, coach owner, reforecast weighted amount.
