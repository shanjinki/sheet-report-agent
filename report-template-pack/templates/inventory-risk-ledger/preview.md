# Inventory Risk Ledger

Use for inventory and warehouse tables where stockout, overstock, and movement exceptions are central.

## Best Fit

- ERP inventory exports
- warehouse stock snapshots
- SKU movement tables
- procurement inventory reviews

## Report Modules

1. SKU count, inventory value, stockout and overstock risk
2. Warehouse/category concentration
3. Movement trend if dates exist
4. Stockout and long-tail overstock exception list
5. Supplier or warehouse risk
6. Replenishment and clearance actions

## Visual Grammar

Ops-ledger style: dark or ledger-like surface, tabular spacing, stock badges, exception strips, green/amber/red risk states.

## Recommendation Style

Operational: replenish, transfer, clear stock, correct data, follow up supplier, revise safety-stock threshold.
