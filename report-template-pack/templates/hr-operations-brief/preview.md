# HR Operations Brief

Use for people operations tables where department comparison and exception follow-up matter.

## Best Fit

- attendance exports
- headcount sheets
- performance summaries
- employee operation trackers

## Report Modules

1. Headcount/record count and key HR metric
2. Department or role distribution
3. Attendance/performance distribution
4. Exception list
5. Repeated issue or manager follow-up list
6. People operations recommendations

## Visual Grammar

Editorial-brief style: warm paper tone, calm cards, segment comparison, human-readable notes.

## Recommendation Style

Careful and privacy-aware: validate data, follow up with managers, adjust process, avoid exposing sensitive employee details unnecessarily.
