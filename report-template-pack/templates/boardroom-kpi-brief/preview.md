# Boardroom KPI Brief

Use for clean executive summaries where readability and confidence matter more than visual drama.

## Best Fit

- finance summaries
- CRM pipeline summaries
- department performance reviews
- generic management reporting

## Report Modules

1. Executive memo headline
2. KPI cards with variance or change notes
3. Ranked drivers table
4. Trend or mix chart
5. Exceptions / risks
6. Next actions and assumptions

## Visual Grammar

Light background, navy/slate text, restrained green/amber/red status colors, crisp cards, simple bar charts, professional spacing.

## Recommendation Style

Clear and measured: what changed, why it matters, what to review next, which owner or department should act.
